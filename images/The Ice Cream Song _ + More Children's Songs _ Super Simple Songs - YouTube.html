It's "The Ice Cream Song" from Super Simple Songs! Many people have asked us for an ice cream song for kids over the years and here it is! In our ice cream song, kids also get to practice counting by counting the number of scoops on the ice cream cone. This collection of kids songs also includes "Do You Like Broccoli Ice Cream?", "Who Took The Cookie?", "Do You Like Lasagna Milkshakes?", and many more of our best children's songs and nursery rhymes. How many scoops of ice cream would you like?
